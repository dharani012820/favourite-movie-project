🎬 MY FAVOURITE MOVIE 🎬

Oh Kadhal Kanmani


📖 About the Movie

Oh Kadhal Kanmani is a beautiful romantic movie directed by Mani Ratnam. The story is about two young people, Adi and Tara, who meet in Mumbai and slowly fall in love. They both have big dreams in life and want to focus on their careers. Instead of getting married immediately, they decide to live together and understand each other better. The movie shows how love can exist without restrictions and how relationships work in modern times. It is simple, realistic, and full of emotions that connect with the audience.


💖 Why I Like This Movie

I like this movie because it shows love in a very natural and realistic way. The characters behave like real people, not like typical cinema characters. Their conversations, fights, and happy moments feel very genuine. This movie teaches that love is not only about staying together, but also about understanding each other, respecting space, and supporting dreams. The emotional scenes are very touching and stay in my heart.


🎭 Main Characters

Adi – Played by Dulquer Salmaan, a creative and ambitious game developer.

Tara – Played by Nithya Menen, a strong and independent architect.

Ganapathy – Played by Prakash Raj, who shows a different perspective of love and marriage.

Each character adds meaning and depth to the story.


🎵 About Songs

The songs of this movie are composed by A.R. Rahman and they are one of the biggest highlights. Every song is soft, melodious, and perfectly matches the mood of the scenes. Songs like Mental Manadhil, Kaara Aattakkaara, and Naane Varugiren are very popular. The background music also makes emotional scenes more powerful and memorable.


🎧 Favourite Songs

🎵 Mental Manadhil

🎵 Kaara Aattakkaara

🎵 Naane Varugiren


🌟 Message of the Movie

The movie gives a strong message about modern relationships. It explains that love is not about rules or traditions, but about trust, understanding, and emotional connection. It also shows that two people can grow individually while still loving each other. This message makes the movie very meaningful and relatable to today’s generation.
